Hobbies

July 2026

Coding

Sport Times Tracking Website

This past vacation, I used my coding expertise learnt at university to solve a problem. My sprinting training group needed a way to track training times across a group of at least 15 people. I decided to use my skills to create a website for that purpose. The website included a JavaScript backend and HTML/CSS frontend. The website is also usable on mobile with great mobile responsiveness in mind. The project has various relevant features including: A decathlon and heptathlon points calulator, a tempo calculator, messages feature to communicate with the coach, a training calendar (locally interchangeable between English and Afrikaans), a training volume metric tracker, and lastly a competitions tab to track performance at various meets.
